Welcome

We are

part of a global Protestant church with a long history. Seventh-day Adventists have been present in Germany since 1876. Through our faith in God and our confession of Jesus Christ, we see ourselves united with Christians around the world. Our faith follows the Reformation principle “Sola Scriptura”, meaning that the Bible alone is the authority and guide for our beliefs. The ancient stories of the Bible remain surprisingly relevant today. They show God’s work throughout history and open a hopeful perspective for the future. A distinctive element of Seventh-day Adventist faith is the weekly observance of the biblical day of rest on Saturday, known as the Sabbath. The word “Sabbath” means to stop or to rest. It is a special time set aside for worship, rest, and fellowship. We also practice baptism for those who personally choose to follow Jesus Christ. Baptism marks the beginning of a believer’s life in Christ and their belonging to the church community. Our church community in Berlin-Waldfriede has been located next to the Waldfriede Hospital since its founding in 1920, and we warmly welcome anyone interested in joining our services.
